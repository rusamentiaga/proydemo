Pinhole Camera Projection

The image plane is located behind the Center of Projection (COP) at distance $f$. Rays from $P(X,Y,Z)$ pass through the origin to hit the plane at $p(x,y,-f)$.

1. Y-Z Similar Triangles (Amber)

$$ \frac{y}{f} = \frac{Y}{Z} \quad \implies \quad y = f \frac{Y}{Z} $$

2. X-Z Similar Triangles (Cyan)

$$ \frac{x}{f} = \frac{X}{Z} \quad \implies \quad x = f \frac{X}{Z} $$

The projected coordinate values are inverted due to the ray passing through the COP.
